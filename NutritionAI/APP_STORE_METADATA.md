### Notes for Review
NutritionAI uses the device camera to capture photos of meals for AI-powered nutritional analysis. The app requires:
- Camera access: To capture meal photos
- Internet connection: To process images through our AI service

The app can be used in two modes:
1. **Guest Mode**: No registration required, data stored locally only
2. **Registered User**: Full features including cloud sync and meal history

The AI analysis is performed by Google's Gemini API on our secure backend servers.
